Header

Target Audience

The target audience is for motorcycle owners or those that may be looking to own a motorcycle.

Client and User Needs

The header is designed to be simple and easy to navigate which is beneficial for the client and user. If users can navigate the site easy they may stay on the site long enough to make a purchase. The contacts for social media, which is normally seen in the footer is located here inside the header. This allows the user to immediately connect more with the client without scrolling the page.

Critique

The color orange that is being used in the header gives off warmth and happiness, combining the physical energy and stimulation of red with the cheerfulness of yellow. This makes it agressive creating a call to action. The white fonts being used balances the orange by giving off a clean and pure feel.

Component 2

CTA

Target Audience

The target audience are those who are athletic and participate physical sports.

Client and User Needs

The use of this CTA is designed so that the background color would not distract the user from the reading the Tag Line. Once you hover over the button the background color shows up. The technique is called a ghost button.

Critique

Once the CTA button gets hovered over a blue color shows up as the background. The blue gives off a feeling of security and trust. This could help the user feel like they made the right choice to continue.
